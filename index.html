Spatiotemporal Trends in Urban Surface Water Quality

From two decades of monitoring, statistical analyses reveal the chemical fingerprints of Eagle Creek, Fall Creek, and the Lower White River. The results highlight both persistent pollution sources and site-level stability, offering lessons in resilience and the importance of long-term monitoring for sustainable water policy.
