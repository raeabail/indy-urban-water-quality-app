Why Does This Map Matter?

It helps us see where water is being tested and how different areas compare. By looking at patterns in the data, scientists can:

  • Spot pollution problems early
  • Connect water quality to land use (like roads, neighborhoods, and farmland)
  • Make better decisions to protect people and nature
